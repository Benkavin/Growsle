Growsle complies fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Our Service is designed exclusively for use by parents and guardians.

When you enter information about your child, that data is:

Security Policy

How we protect your family's data

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at Rest: All stored personal data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • Payment Security: All payments processed by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 certified). We never store full card numbers
  • Access Controls: Employee access to user data is strictly limited, logged, and requires multi-factor authentication
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on AWS with automatic backups, redundancy, and DDoS protection
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security reviews and annual penetration testing

Organisational Safeguards

  • All employees with data access undergo background checks and security training
  • We follow a principle of least privilege — staff only access data necessary for their role
  • All third-party vendors are vetted for security compliance before any data access is granted

Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, Growsle will:

  1. Notify affected users via email within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  2. Notify relevant regulatory authorities as required by applicable law (GDPR, CCPA)
  3. Provide full details of what data was affected and steps being taken
  4. Offer guidance on protective actions you can take immediately

Vulnerability Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@growsle.com. Do not publicly disclose until we have addressed it. We commit to acknowledging your report within 48 hours.

Bug Bounty: We appreciate responsible disclosure and may offer recognition or compensation for significant security findings at our discretion.

Understanding how our AI works and its limitations

Critical: Growsle's AI provides general parenting guidance only. It is not a substitute for professional advice from paediatricians, child psychologists, therapists, or other qualified professionals.

How Our AI Works

Growsle uses large language model (LLM) technology to generate personalised parenting guidance. Our AI is informed by general parenting research, child development literature, and expert resources, then personalised using information you provide about your child.

AI Limitations — What You Must Know

  • Not Always Accurate: AI can make mistakes, misunderstand context, or generate outdated information
  • Not Personalised Medical Advice: The AI does not know your child's full medical history or individual health circumstances

  • Cultural Variance: Guidance reflects general developmental psychology and may not account for all cultural contexts
