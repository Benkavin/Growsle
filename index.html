What Growsle does

Everything you wish
someone had told you.

Six features — each one built around your child's exact age, not a generic range.

I
Age-Exact Daily Guidance
Every morning a personalised tip arrives — calibrated to your child's exact age in weeks. Not "12 to 18 months." What matters for your child, today.
II
"Is This Normal?" Answered
Describe any behaviour — tantrums, sleep shifts, biting, social changes — and receive a calm, research-backed answer in seconds. No more late-night searching.
III
Bespoke Bedtime Stories
A new story every night starring your child's name, favourite things, and a gentle lesson chosen just for them. Bedtime becomes something they ask for.
IV
Weekly Milestone Reports
Know what to watch for this week and get a clear, honest signal when something is worth mentioning to your paediatrician. Confidence, not worry.
V
Evening Reflection
One meaningful question each evening. Over time it builds your parenting confidence and deepens your connection with your child in ways you'll feel.
VI
Every Child, Every Stage
Multiple children — each with their own fully personalised daily experience. No extra effort. Just the right guidance for every child you love.
